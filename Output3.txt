📝 Fresh Fruit Medley:
⏱️ Prep Time: 10 minutes
🍽️ Servings: 4
💪 Nutrition: Rich in vitamins C and fiber
✨ Optional ingredients available: pineapple, orange

📋 Instructions:
1. Wash all fruits thoroughly
2. Cut apples and pears into bite-sized chunks
3. Halve the grapes
4. If using pineapple, cut into small chunks
5. Mix all fruits in a large bowl
6. Optional: Add a squeeze of orange juice to prevent browning
--------------------------------------------------

📝 Protein-Packed Deli Plate:
⏱️ Prep Time: 10 minutes
🍽️ Servings: 2
💪 Nutrition: High in protein and calcium
✨ Optional ingredients available: tomato

📋 Instructions:
1. Arrange sliced ham and cheese on a plate
2. Hard boil eggs, cool, and slice
3. If using, slice cucumber and tomato for garnish
4. Serve with whole grain crackers or bread
--------------------------------------------------

📝 Broccoli and Cheese Bake:
⏱️ Prep Time: 25 minutes
🍽️ Servings: 4
💪 Nutrition: Good source of calcium and vitamin C
✨ Optional ingredients available: carrot

📋 Instructions:
1. Cut broccoli into florets
2. Steam broccoli until tender-crisp
3. Grate cheese
4. If using, slice carrots thinly
5. Layer vegetables in a baking dish
6. Top with cheese and bake until melted
--------------------------------------------------
