📝 Colorful Garden Salad:
⏱️ Prep Time: 15 minutes
🍽️ Servings: 4
💪 Nutrition: High in vitamins A, C, and K

📋 Instructions:

2. Tear lettuce into bite-sized pieces
3. Slice tomatoes, cucumbers, and radishes
4. Grate or julienne carrots
5. If using, slice bell peppers
6. Combine all ingredients
7. Serve with your favorite dressing

📝 Protein-Packed Deli Plate:
⏱️ Prep Time: 10 minutes
🍽️ Servings: 2
💪 Nutrition: High in protein and calcium
✨ Optional ingredients available: cucumber, tomato

📋 Instructions:
